# Illustration Prompts - Hermione Granger Presentation

> **Style guide for all prompts:** Studio Ghibli anime style, soft watercolor textures, warm gentle lighting, whimsical and dreamy atmosphere, rich purple and gold color palette, no text, no logos. The character is the same young bushy-haired witch girl in every image: brown eyes, warm brown bushy hair, wearing a dark Hogwarts-style school robe with a burgundy-and-gold tie.

> **Video guide for all prompts:** Use the corresponding slide image as the input/reference frame. Keep the camera static. Only add subtle, gentle character animation. No camera movement, no zoom, no new elements. Loop-friendly, 3-4 seconds.

---

## Slide 1 — Title Slide

**Image prompt:**
Studio Ghibli anime style illustration of a young bushy-haired witch girl standing confidently in front of a grand magical castle at twilight. She wears a dark Hogwarts-style school robe with a burgundy-and-gold tie. She holds a glowing wand in one hand and a stack of old leather-bound books in the other. Golden sparkles swirl gently around her. The background is a deep purple starry sky with the castle's warm windows glowing in the distance. Soft watercolor textures, warm gentle lighting, whimsical and dreamy atmosphere, rich purple and gold color palette, no text.

**Video prompt:**
Subtle animation of the girl's hair and robe gently swaying in a light breeze. The golden sparkles around her drift slowly upward. The wand tip glows with a faint pulsing light. Stars in the sky twinkle softly. Static camera, no zoom, gentle looping motion.

## Slide 7 — Hermione's World (Hogwarts Legacy)

**Image prompt:**
Studio Ghibli anime style illustration of two hands holding a handheld gaming console (Steam Deck style) in the foreground. On the console screen, a young bushy-haired witch girl in a dark Hogwarts-style school robe with a burgundy-and-gold tie is sitting in a grand library, waving excitedly. An owl sits on the table beside her next to an open book. Gothic arched windows and tall bookshelves fill the in-game background with warm purple-golden light. Behind the console, a real window shows a suburban view with green trees and apartment buildings. Soft watercolor textures, warm gentle lighting, whimsical and dreamy atmosphere, no text.

**Video prompt:**
On the console screen, the witch girl waves her hand gently back and forth. The owl beside her blinks and tilts its head. Faint golden sparkles drift across the in-game library scene. The pages of the open book flutter slightly. The hands holding the console stay mostly still with a very subtle natural sway. The real-world background outside the window remains static. Static camera, no zoom, gentle looping motion.
